Deplatformr liberates your data

  1. Select a content platform from the menu on the left.
  2. Follow instructions on how to export and deplatform your content.
  3. Upload your content to Deplatformr.
  4. Deplatformr will re-process your content and move it to decentralized storage on the Filecoin network.
  5. All your metadata, content, and AI models are encrypted. Only you have access to the decryption keys.
  6. You can view and manage content from each individual platform using the menus on the left.
  7. You can also access all your files from across all of the platforms via the Filecoin menu.
  8. Export your Filecoin information. You can always retrieve the files from the decentralized network yourself if you no longer want to use Deplatformr.
  9. Your Deplatformr subscription pays for storing content on Filecoin. Click on your user name in the top right menu to view and/or top up your account balance.
  10. Click on the Filecoin menu to see information about the storage deals that Deplatformr has made for you on the Filecoin network, along with your current Filecoin wallet balance.
